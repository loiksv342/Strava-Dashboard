About this project

Strava Personal Dashboard is a Flask-based web application that connects to your Strava account and visualizes your training data in a clean, interactive dashboard. The goal of the project is to help individual athletes analyze their performance across running and cycling disciplines, track trends over time, and discover personal bests on standard distances. Key Features: OAuth2 authentication with Strava Fetches and displays your latest activities (runs and rides) Calculates best efforts for common distances (1 km, 1 mile, 5 km, half marathon, marathon for running; 1 km, 5 km, 10 km, 20 km, 50 km, 100 km for cycling) Interactive charts for distance, speed, duration, and heart rate using Chart.js Filter options to explore your training over different time ranges Clean frontend with HTML/CSS/JS and Bootstrap 5 The application is built with Python (Flask), PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, and modern frontend tools. It serves as both a learning project and a practical dashboard for athletes who want better insights into their performance.
